Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into cubes
  • 200g plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin, paprika, and salt. Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a skillet until browned and cooked through, then set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  4. Add canned tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in coconut milk and the cooked chicken, simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice or naan and garnish with fresh coriander.
