Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Salt, for pasta water

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the cheeses and black pepper.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some pasta water and drain.
  5. Quickly mix the hot pasta with the pancetta and then stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.
