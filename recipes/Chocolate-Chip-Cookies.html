Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  • In a bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  • Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  • In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to the butter mixture.
  • Stir in the chocolate chips.
  • Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
  • Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin, paprika, and salt. Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken in a skillet until browned and cooked through, then set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  4. Add canned tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in coconut milk and the cooked chicken, simmer for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice or naan and garnish with fresh coriander.
